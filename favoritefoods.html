Danna's Favorite Foods

Pasta

Pasta is my first favorite food.
It is so good and there are many different kinds of pastas.
Some of my top three favorites are
Carbonara, Spaghetti and Oil Base.
Pasta is so good and if you haven't eaten it before
you have to! Since you are missing out.


Ribs

Ribs are my second favorite food.
Most times I would eat it without any sauce
but when I do, I pair it with bbq sauce.
My favorite ribs are the ones that my mom makes.
There's just something that makes it taste
better than all the other ribs I have eaten in my life.
Ribs are really good and if you haven't eaten it
you are missing out (again).


Baked Sushi

Baked Sushi is my third favorite food.
My mom makes these and of course they are my favorite.
I don't like sushi that much but the way my
mom makes the baked sushi has changed that.
It's so tasty and it has that flavor that
makes me feel warm and good inside.

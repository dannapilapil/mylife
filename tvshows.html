Danna's Favorite TV Shows

The Flash

The Flash is such an awesome and exciting show.
It's about the fastest man on earth and his purpose is to
protect people from the meta-humans (people who have powers).
As Barry Allen continues to save people he learns new things and becomes faster.
This show is part of the DC Comics and is pretty interesting every time I watch it.


Arrow

Arrow is such a thrilling and action-packed show.
In my opinion, Arrow is better than the Flash.
Arrow is more serious and sophisticated.
Oliver Queen has been through some really tough times and
during those times he had to do things that he truly regret.
Now that he is back home he is a changed person and protects his city from criminals.
This show is also part of the DC Comics universe.

How To Get Away With Murder

How To Get Away With Murder is such a suspenseful and mind twisting show.
Annalise Keating is a professor of defense law and teaches
a class called How to Get Away With Murder.
She is also a criminal defense attorney, and she selects a group of students.
The students Annalise picked are very bright and they are the best.
They are there to assist with the cases at Annalise's firm.
The show is full of surprises and twists. It is a must see!

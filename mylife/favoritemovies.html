Danna's Favorite Movies

Interstellar

Interstellar has such an amazing and thorough plot. The casting for this movie was really well put together. Also, the animation and special effects of this movie was very creative and breathtaking. I really recommend to see this movie if you haven't.


The Martian

The Martian is a very interesting and well thought out movie. Some of the cast were actually from Interstellar and they also played the roles of astronauts. The plot line for this movie was remarkable and very eye-opening. This movie is a must see if you haven't yet.

Danna's Favorite Movies

Interstellar

Interstellar has such an amazing and thorough plot.
The casting for this movie was really well put together.
Also, the animation and special effects of this movie was very creative and breathtaking.
I really recommend to see this movie if you haven't.


The Martian

The Martian is a very interesting and well thought out movie.
Some of the cast were actually from Interstellar and they also played the roles of astronauts.
The plot line for this movie was remarkable and very eye-opening.
This movie is a must see if you haven't yet.


Star Wars: The Force Awakens

Star Wars: The Force Awakens is such a thrilling and exciting movie.
It is part of the epic space film series created by George Lucas.
The movie has an amazing soundtrack.
Featuring the theme song of the film; which gives me goosebumps when I hear it.
This movie has everything a movie needs.
You have to see this movie-even if you haven't seen the other ones.
